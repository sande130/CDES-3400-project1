Bridge the gap between art history and today’s popular media!

Exhibition Details

Enter the exciting and visually rich world of Pop-History, a highly interactive traveling exhibition that joins art history with todays popular media.

From the 14th century renaissance to today’s videogames, visitors will explore the exhibit surrounded by colorful, larger-than-life media. In this stimulating and fun environment, families and children will delight in bringing their own creations to life as they experiment with storyboarding, character design, drawing techniques, movement, timing, filming, and sound.

About Us

Our Mission:

we want to help usher children into the world of fine art by showing them how it connects with the popular media they love.

Why we do what we do:

Children can often feel “forced” to learn about fine art. We feel that part of the problem is the separation between fine art and the present art forms they are already interested in. By relating what they are familiar with to the historical side of art, we show them the influences the art of the past has had on their current media.
